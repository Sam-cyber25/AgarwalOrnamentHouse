Our Story

Founder's Legacy

A Legacy Built on Trust

Founded in 1947 in the heart of Kanpur, Agarwal Ornament House began with a simple promise: offer jewellery of unquestionable purity, crafted with care, and presented with honesty. That promise still defines us today.

Over the decades, our showroom in Nayaganj has welcomed generations of families searching for bridal heirlooms, celebration pieces, and trusted everyday jewellery. Our reputation has grown through referrals, relationships, and consistency.

As 916 certified wholesalers and retailers, we remain committed to hallmarked quality, timeless craftsmanship, and a buying experience rooted in heritage, not hype.

Our Journey

78 Years of Milestones

1947

A Heritage Begins

Agarwal Ornament House opens its doors in Nayaganj, Kanpur with a focus on trust, purity, and family relationships.

1960s

A Trusted Family Name

The showroom becomes a known destination for wedding jewellery and fine traditional pieces across local communities.

1980s

Expanded Craftsmanship

The collection expands to include more bridal, silver, and gemstone-led designs while keeping personal service at the center.

2000s

Hallmarked Confidence

Hallmark-first thinking strengthens customer assurance and positions the brand for a new generation of quality-conscious buyers.

2010s

Broader Jewellery Range

Gold, silver, diamond, and pearl offerings grow under one roof for wholesale and retail enquiries alike.

Today

Legacy in Motion

Four generations of trust continue to guide every conversation, every showcase, and every hallmarked piece offered to Kanpur families.
